Frequently Asked Questions

TeknoyMart is an e-commerce platform specializing in technology and electronics. We offer a wide range of products including computers, smartphones, accessories, and more.

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our homepage. Fill in the required information, including your name, email address, and password. Once completed, you'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

TeknoyMart accepts various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. We ensure secure transactions for all payment options.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in their original condition and packaging. Some exceptions apply for certain electronics. Please check our detailed return policy page for more information.

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website's "Track Order" page or directly on the courier's website to monitor your package's status and estimated delivery date.
